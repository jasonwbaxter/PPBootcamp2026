# Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have a question about the bootcamp content, a bug to report, or just want to say hello, here are the best ways to reach us.

## GitHub

The fastest way to start a conversation is to open an issue on our repository:

1. Visit the [PPBootcamp2026 repository](https://github.com/jasonwbaxter/PPBootcamp2026)
2. Click **Issues** → **New issue**
3. Choose the appropriate template and describe your question or feedback

## Email

For private inquiries, you can reach us at:

> **bootcamp@example.com**

We aim to respond within two business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I contribute a new post or page?**
Copy the relevant template from the `templates/` folder, add your content, and open a pull request.

**Q: I found a broken link — what should I do?**
Please open a GitHub issue with the page URL and the broken link so we can fix it quickly.

**Q: Can I reuse content from this site?**
Yes! All content is published under the [MIT License](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT) unless otherwise noted.
